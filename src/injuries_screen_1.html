Common Injuries in Skateboarding
Head Injuries Several hed injuries include concussions, blunt trauma, skull fractures. They make up 20% of all skateboarding injuries

Face Injuries
Common injuries to the face include broken noses and jaws, cuts and scrapes, which take 16% of skateboarding injuries.

Broken Wrist Most common. Injuries to the extremities, which includes broken wrists, broken wrists, broken wrists make up 19%.

Long bone fractures The most prone to injury, 34% injuries are long bone fractures five sets of long bones include thigh bone, lower leg and shin bones, upper arm, radius, forearm bones.

Ankle injuries
Range from ankle sprains and even broken ankles. They make up 11% of skateboarding injuries.
